About Rasoiyaa Food

Swad Apki Apni Rasoi Ka

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide healthy, fresh and homemade snacks with high quality, hygiene and at affordable prices.

We believe that everyone deserves to experience the authentic taste of traditional Indian snacks, made with love and care, just like in your own kitchen.

Our Story

Based in Satna, Madhya Pradesh, our snacks brand specializes in ready-to-fry chips, papads, and ready-to-serve snacks. We prioritize quality and flavor to enrich snacking experiences.

Our varied offerings include banana chips, chivda, murmura, and namak para, ensuring there's something for everyone.

"Rasoiyaa ek chhoti si soch se shuru hua tha – ghar jaisa swad sab tak pahunchane ka sapna. Aaj hum har snack ke saath wahi ghar ka pyaar, purity aur flavour aapke ghar tak le aate hain. Ye sirf snack nahi, ek experience hai jo har din ko special banata hai."

Our Values

Quality

We use only the finest ingredients and traditional recipes to ensure every snack meets our high standards.

Freshness

All our snacks are made fresh daily using traditional methods to preserve authentic flavors.

Homemade

Every product is crafted with the same love and care you'd find in a home kitchen.

Affordable

We believe authentic taste shouldn't come at a premium - our snacks are priced for everyone.

Meet Our Team

Passionate About Food

Our team consists of passionate individuals who share a common love for traditional Indian cuisine and snacks. Each member brings their expertise and dedication to ensure that every product that leaves our kitchen meets the highest standards of quality and taste.

From our master chefs who follow age-old recipes to our quality control team that ensures freshness and hygiene, everyone at Rasoiyaa Food is committed to bringing you the best snacking experience.

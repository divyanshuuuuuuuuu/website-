Policy Overview

At Rasoiyaa Food, we are committed to delivering your fresh Indian snacks safely and on time. This Shipping & Delivery Policy outlines our shipping procedures, delivery areas, charges, and timelines.

Our Delivery Promise

We ensure that your orders are delivered fresh and on time. All products are carefully packaged to maintain quality during transit.

Our Commitment

  • Fresh, high-quality products delivered safely
  • Transparent shipping charges and delivery timelines
  • Real-time order tracking
  • Reliable delivery partners

Delivery Areas

Primary Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to the following areas:

  • Madhya Pradesh: Satna and surrounding areas
  • Delhi NCR: Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Ghaziabad
  • Maharashtra: Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur
  • Uttar Pradesh: Lucknow, Kanpur, Allahabad
  • Rajasthan: Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur

Service Expansion

We are continuously expanding our delivery network. If your area is not listed, please contact us for updates on service availability.

Delivery Coverage

Currently serving 50+ cities across India. Contact us to check if we deliver to your location.

Order Processing

Processing Steps

Every order goes through our quality control process:

  1. Order Verification: Check order details and payment
  2. Quality Check: Inspect products for freshness
  3. Packaging: Secure packaging with temperature control
  4. Labeling: Accurate labeling and documentation
  5. Dispatch: Handover to delivery partner

Cut-off Times

  • Same-day processing: Orders placed before 2 PM
  • Next-day processing: Orders placed after 2 PM
  • Weekend orders: Processed on Monday

Order Holds

Orders may be temporarily held for:

  • Payment verification
  • Address confirmation
  • Product availability issues
  • Quality control checks

Packaging & Safety

Packaging Standards

We use food-grade packaging materials:

  • Temperature-controlled packaging: For perishable items
  • Air-tight containers: To maintain freshness
  • Shock-absorbent materials: To prevent damage
  • Eco-friendly materials: Sustainable packaging

Food Safety Measures

  • Hygiene-certified packaging facility
  • Temperature monitoring during transit
  • Freshness guarantee on all products
  • Quality checks before dispatch

Environmental Responsibility

We are committed to sustainable packaging:

  • Recyclable materials wherever possible
  • Minimal plastic usage
  • Biodegradable packaging options
  • Reduced carbon footprint initiatives

Shipping Restrictions

Restricted Items

Due to food safety regulations, we cannot ship:

  • Perishable items requiring refrigeration
  • Liquid or semi-liquid food items
  • Items requiring special temperature control
  • Custom-made or personalized products

Address Restrictions

  • PO Box addresses
  • Military or restricted areas
  • Remote locations without proper addressing
  • International addresses (currently)

Weather-Related Delays

Shipping may be delayed due to:

  • Heavy rainfall or flooding
  • Snow or extreme weather conditions
  • Natural disasters or calamities
  • Government restrictions or lockdowns

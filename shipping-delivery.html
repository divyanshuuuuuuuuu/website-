Shipping & Delivery Policy

We ensure timely and safe delivery of your orders. Here's what you need to know.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to Delhi-NCR and surrounding areas. Delivery outside this region may incur additional charges.

Delivery Time

Orders placed before 2 PM are delivered the same day. Orders after 2 PM are delivered the next day.

Shipping Charges

Free delivery for orders above ₹500. For orders below ₹500, a delivery charge of ₹50 applies.

Packaging

All products are packaged with care to ensure freshness and prevent damage during transit.

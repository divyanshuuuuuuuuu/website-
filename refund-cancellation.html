Policy Overview

At Rasoiyaa Food, we are committed to providing you with high-quality Indian snacks and excellent customer service. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines our procedures for order cancellations, returns, and refunds.

Important Notice

Due to the perishable nature of food products, we have specific policies for returns and refunds. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

Our Commitment

  • Fresh, high-quality products delivered to your doorstep
  • Fair and transparent refund and cancellation policies
  • Quick resolution of quality issues
  • Responsive customer support

Order Cancellation

Pre-Shipment Cancellation

You may cancel your order before it is shipped:

  • Within 1 hour: Full refund with no questions asked
  • 1-24 hours: Cancellation subject to order status
  • After 24 hours: Order may already be in processing

How to Cancel

To cancel your order:

  1. Contact us immediately at rasoiyaafood@gmail.com
  2. Call us at +91 8085654059
  3. Provide your order number and reason for cancellation
  4. We will confirm cancellation and process refund

Return Eligibility

Due to the nature of food products, returns are limited to specific circumstances:

Eligible for Return

  • Damaged Products: Items damaged during shipping
  • Defective Products: Products with manufacturing defects
  • Wrong Items: Items different from what was ordered
  • Expired Products: Items past their expiration date
  • Quality Issues: Products not meeting quality standards

Return Conditions

  • Return request must be made within 24 hours of delivery
  • Products must be in original packaging
  • Products must be unopened and unused
  • Photographic evidence may be required
  • Return shipping costs are borne by the customer

Important

We cannot accept returns for products that have been opened, consumed, or damaged by the customer.

Return Process

Step 1: Contact Us

Immediately contact our customer support:

  • Email: rasoiyaafood@gmail.com
  • Phone: +91 8085654059
  • WhatsApp: +91 8085654059

Step 2: Provide Information

Please provide the following information:

  • Order number
  • Product name and quantity
  • Reason for return
  • Photographs of the issue (if applicable)
  • Preferred resolution (refund or replacement)

Step 3: Return Authorization

We will review your request and provide:

  • Return authorization number
  • Return shipping address
  • Return instructions
  • Expected timeline for resolution

Step 4: Ship Return

Package the items securely and ship to the provided address:

  • Use original packaging when possible
  • Include return authorization number
  • Use trackable shipping method
  • Keep shipping receipt for reference

Refund Process

Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to original card (5-7 business days)
  • UPI: Refunded to UPI account (2-3 business days)
  • Net Banking: Refunded to bank account (3-5 business days)
  • Digital Wallets: Refunded to wallet account (1-2 business days)

Refund Amount

The refund amount will include:

  • Full product price for eligible returns
  • Original shipping charges (if applicable)
  • Processing fees may be deducted for certain cases

Refund Timeline

  • Return received: 1-2 business days
  • Quality inspection: 1-2 business days
  • Refund processing: 2-5 business days
  • Total time: 5-10 business days

Quality Issues

We take product quality seriously and have specific procedures for quality-related issues:

Immediate Resolution

For quality issues, we offer immediate resolution:

  • Instant refund for confirmed quality issues
  • Free replacement if preferred
  • Store credit for future purchases
  • No return shipping required for quality issues

Quality Standards

Our products must meet these standards:

  • Fresh and properly packaged
  • Within expiration date
  • Free from contamination
  • Properly sealed containers
  • Accurate product descriptions

Documentation Required

For quality issues, please provide:

  • Clear photographs of the issue
  • Product batch/lot number (if available)
  • Purchase date and order number
  • Description of the quality issue
